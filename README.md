# Deep Neural Networks in PyTorch

## About

Welcome to the "Deep Neural Networks in PyTorch" repository. Here, we explore the fascinating world of Deep Neural Networks (DNNs) using the powerful PyTorch framework. Whether you're a novice looking to understand the basics or an experienced practitioner seeking to fine-tune your skills, this project is designed to empower your journey into the realm of deep learning.

### What's Inside

- **Fundamentals**: Delve into the foundational concepts of DNNs and CNNs, from neurons to layers, activation functions to loss functions.

- **PyTorch Primer**: Get acquainted with PyTorch, one of the most popular deep learning libraries, through hands-on examples and tutorials.

- **Custom Networks**: Build your own neural network architectures using PyTorch, allowing you to tailor models to your specific needs.

- **Hyperparameter Optimization**: Discover techniques to optimize the performance of your models by fine-tuning hyperparameters.
